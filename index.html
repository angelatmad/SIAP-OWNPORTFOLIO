ABOUT ME

I am a dedicated BSIT student and an aspiring Front-End Developer with a strong passion for crafting clean, modern, and minimalist design. I specialize in building simple, functional websites using core technologies: HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.

Driven by continuous improvement, I am actively expanding my technical toolkit to include visual prototyping in Figma and exploring backend logic with PHP and Java. Beyond the code, my creative pursuits—including drawing, singing, and a unique appreciation for the color pink—fuel my attention to detail and design aesthetic in every project.
